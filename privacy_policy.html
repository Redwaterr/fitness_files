Privacy Policy for Fit Life

Last Updated: 12/10/2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and fitness-related data to provide you with personalized services. The information we collect includes:

  • Personal Data: Name, age, gender, height, weight, and other data required to personalize your experience.
  • Fitness Data: Fitness level, fitness goals, workout preferences, and other health-related information.
  • Usage Data: Information related to how you interact with our app, including features you use, the time spent on the app, and your preferences.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to offer you personalized features and improve your overall experience. This includes:

  • Creating customized workout plans and diet plans based on your goals and fitness level.
  • Providing personalized healthy recipes and workouts.
  • Tailoring essays and other educational content to suit your fitness and health goals.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in running the app, such as cloud storage and analytics providers. These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and may not use your data for any other purpose.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no security system is completely foolproof, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal requirements.
  • Withdraw consent to data processing where applicable.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance your experience on our app. These tools help us understand how users interact with the app and enable us to improve our services.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
